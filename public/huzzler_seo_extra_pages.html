alt="Freelancer working on a laptop, connected to clients on Huzzler"
Describe the scene; avoid generic "hero image"

alt="AI-powered matching icon for Huzzler freelance search"

Alt text tests
Category icons
alt="" (decorative — category name is the visible label below)
Article screenshot
alt="Screenshot showing the [feature name] settings screen on Huzzler"
Be specific about what part of the UI is shown
Step-by-step images
alt="Step [N]: [What is happening in this step] on Huzzler"
Numbered steps in alt text help screen reader users follow along
Search bar illustration
alt="Search the Huzzler Help Center for answers"
Help articles are high-value SEO pages. Each article should target a specific question (e.g. "how to withdraw earnings on Huzzler"). Use FAQPage JSON-LD schema on FAQ-style articles to get Google rich results (accordion snippets in search).
